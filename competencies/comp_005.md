title: Normalize a schema
miller_level: knows_how
parent_skill: skill_database_design
---

Normalization is the process of structuring tables to reduce data redundancy. A
functional dependency describes how one attribute determines the value of
another attribute. First, second and third normal form impose progressively
stricter rules on table structure. Decomposition splits a table into smaller
tables to remove insertion, update and deletion anomalies. A well normalized
schema stores each fact once and links its tables through keys.
