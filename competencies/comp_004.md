title: Aggregate and group data
miller_level: knows_how
parent_skill: skill_query_construction
---

Aggregation summarizes many rows into a single computed value. The GROUP BY
clause partitions rows into groups that share a common column value. Aggregate
functions such as count, sum, average, minimum and maximum compute one result
per group. The HAVING clause filters groups after aggregation, unlike WHERE
which filters individual rows. Grouped queries turn detailed table data into
compact summary results.
